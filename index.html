Our Courses

Learn about the available courses at MY9 and choose the one that suits you best

General English

  • The course covers 4 levels (from Beginner to Intermediate), each lasting 3 months.

  • Classes are held 3 times a week for 80 minutes, using the OXFORD SOLUTION program from Oxford University Press.

  • Students are provided with free textbooks and individual workbooks.

  • The price includes teacher support for missed lessons or difficulties.

IELTS Standard

  • Gain basic knowledge about the IELTS format.

  • Learn strategies for successfully passing the IELTS test.

  • Participate in mock tests to simulate the real IELTS exam.

  • Each class lasts 80 minutes, ensuring optimal engagement in learning.

  • The price includes teacher support for missed lessons or difficulties.
